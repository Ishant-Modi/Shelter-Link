Who We Are?

MigrantConnect is a digital platform designed to address the challenges faced by migrant workers in India. We provide a secure, transparent ecosystem that connects workers with verified employers, ensures fair wages, and facilitates access to government welfare schemes.

  • Secure Aadhaar-based verification
  • AI-powered job matching
  • Real-time grievance redressal
  • Access to welfare schemes

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for migrant workers

Aadhaar-Based Registration

Secure identity verification using Aadhaar ensures authentic profiles for both workers and employers.

Job Matching & Tracking

AI-powered job matching connects workers with suitable employment opportunities based on skills and location.

Welfare Scheme Access

Simplified access to government welfare schemes with eligibility checking and application assistance.

Grievance Redressal

Real-time grievance filing and tracking system to ensure fair treatment of workers.

Wage Transparency

Transparent wage agreements and payment tracking to ensure timely and fair compensation.

QR Identity

Digital identity cards with QR codes for easy verification and access to services.
