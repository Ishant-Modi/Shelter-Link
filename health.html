Health Tips for Migrant Workers

Workplace Safety

  • • Always wear protective gear at construction sites
  • • Stay hydrated during hot weather
  • • Take regular breaks from heavy work
  • • Report unsafe working conditions

Nutrition & Diet

  • • Eat balanced meals with proteins and vitamins
  • • Drink clean and safe water
  • • Avoid street food if possible
  • • Include fruits and vegetables in diet

Rest & Recovery

  • • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours daily)
  • • Maintain clean living conditions
  • • Practice stress management techniques
  • • Regular exercise and stretching

Emergency Preparedness

  • • Keep emergency contact numbers handy
  • • Know the location of nearest hospital
  • • Carry health insurance cards always
  • • Learn basic first aid techniques
